Nicolae Testemițanu State University of Medicine and Pharmacy

he Chişinău State University of Pharmacy and Medicine was established as part of the Medicine Institute nr. 2 from Saint Petersburg, which was evacuated during the second World War , which was later transferred to Chişinău together with the students and the teaching staff under the name of the State Institute of Medicine, Since 1990 the Institute has the name of Nicolea testemitanu. On July 25, 1991 the State Institute of Medicine “Nicolae Testemiţnu” was reorganised as a university. In 1995, it was named – The State University of Medicine and Pharmacy “Nicolae Testemiţanu”
